New Our Education

In the 1914-1915 academic year in Russia, 127.4 thousand people studied in 105 universities [1. In the 1970-1971 academic year, 4.6 million students studied at 805 universities in the Soviet Union. Of these, in engineering and economic specialties - 43.6%, humanitarian and natural sciences - 35.6%, agricultural - 7.7%, medical - 7.1%. 327.2 thousand scientific and pedagogical workers were involved in their training, including 10.4 thousand doctors and 95.3 thousand candidates of sciences[1].

The purpose of education is the formation of knowledge, skills and intellectual, moral, creative and physical development of the student's personality.
